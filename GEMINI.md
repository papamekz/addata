# Gemini Instructions

Use `AGENTS.md` as the primary operating instructions for this repository.

For OOH/DOOH analysis, apply the structured assessment protocol in `SKILL.md`
before forming conclusions. Use `data/digest.json` for compact retrieval,
`data/index.json` for full machine-readable claim metadata, and
`data/rag-chunks.jsonl` for RAG workflows.

Check `DATA_QUALITY.md` before high-stakes use. Treat advertising quote records
as cultural or ethical context, not empirical evidence.
